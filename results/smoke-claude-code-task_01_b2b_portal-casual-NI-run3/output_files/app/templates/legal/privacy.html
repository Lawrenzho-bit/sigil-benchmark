Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-19. This is a template — review with counsel before production use.

1. Who we are

Acme Portal ("we", "us") provides a B2B SaaS portal. We act as a data processor for content your organization stores, and as a data controller for account and billing data.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: name, email, hashed password, organization membership.
  • Usage data: sign-in timestamps, IP address, and user agent, recorded in the audit log.
  • Billing data: plan and subscription status. Card details are handled solely by Stripe; we never see or store them.

3. How we use it

To operate the service, authenticate you, secure your account, prevent abuse, process payments, and meet legal obligations. We send transactional email (security, billing, invitations). Marketing email is sent only with your explicit, separately-given consent.

4. Cookies

We set one strictly-necessary cookie that keeps you signed in and protects forms against CSRF. We do not set advertising or analytics cookies. The consent banner records your choice in a further functional cookie.

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

Performance of a contract (providing the service), legitimate interests (security and abuse prevention), consent (marketing email), and legal obligation (tax and accounting records).

6. Your rights

You can access, export, correct, and delete your personal data. Use Data & privacy to download a full export or to delete your account. You may also lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

7. Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. Audit records are retained for security and compliance and may outlive an account in anonymized form. Deleting your account removes your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

8. Sub-processors

Stripe (payments) and our email delivery provider. Each processes data only as needed to provide their service.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@acme.example.
