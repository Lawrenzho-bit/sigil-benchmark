Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

This policy explains what personal data {{ app_name }} ("we") collects, why, and the rights you have under the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data we collect

  • Account data — your name, email address, and hashed password.
  • Organization data — the organizations you belong to and your role.
  • Security data — sign-in timestamps, IP addresses, and audit-log entries, kept to protect your account and meet our compliance obligations.
  • Billing data — handled by our payment processor, Stripe; we store only a customer reference and subscription status, never card numbers.

Why we process it

To provide the portal (contract performance), to keep it secure and meet legal obligations (legitimate interest / legal obligation), and — only with your explicit consent — to send product updates.

Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. Audit-log entries are retained for compliance even after an account is deleted, but are disassociated from your identifying details.

Your rights

You can access and export your data, rectify it, and erase your account at any time from Data & privacy in your account settings. You may also withdraw marketing consent there. To exercise any other right, contact privacy@example.com.

Sub-processors

We use Stripe (payments) and our hosting and email-delivery providers. Each processes data only on our instructions.

See also our Cookie Policy and Terms of Service.
